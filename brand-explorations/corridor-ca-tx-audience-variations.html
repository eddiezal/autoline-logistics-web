Enclosed transport questions

What does enclosed actually mean?

Hard-sided trailer (not soft-side), climate-controlled cabin, lift-gate loading, soft-tie tie-downs (not chains). The car never touches weather.

What does your $500K coverage mean?

Contingent cargo coverage that sits above the carrier's primary policy. If a loss exceeds carrier limits, we cover up to $500K. Most brokers carry only bond minimum.

Will I get pickup and delivery photos?

Yes. Pre-load, post-load, in-transit at every fuel stop, pre-delivery, post-delivery. Photos arrive in your portal in near real-time.

How is enclosed priced vs open?

Roughly 40-60 percent more than open for the same lane. The price difference is the trailer, not the route. Coordinator confirms exact pricing at quote.

Do you handle dealer paperwork?

Yes. We work with finance, leasing, and dealer titling. Send the dealer contact at booking and our coordinator owns the paperwork loop.
