My take

A1 is the right Phase A play. It preserves the at-a-glance proof the critic asked for, mirrors the actual tool's design so the click-through feels like a continuation, and is the lowest-build of the four (mostly a restyle of what's already deployed).

C is the cleanest but loses the critic's biggest point. A2 is what's deployed but visually doesn't match the tool, which is the issue you flagged.
