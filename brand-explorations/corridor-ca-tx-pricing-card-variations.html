My take

V1 or V4. V1 if you want to preserve the verdict-box visual language we built (just smaller). V4 if you want maximum compactness and the "Personalize →" link gets people into /tools/ship-vs-drive (which then funnels back to /quote).

V3 is the most aggressive for paid ads — one number does all the work. But it underweights SUV and Pickup, which are real buyer segments. Worth considering if the LA paid test ads target "California to Texas car shipping cost" and we want the number to be the headline.

V2 reads more like a dashboard than a marketing card. The 3-column layout is harder to scan when prices have different decimal lengths ($900–$1,100 vs $1,260–$1,540 don't align).
